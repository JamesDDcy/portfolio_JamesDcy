SUPERPOT

The SuperPot is an Internet of Things project, which aims to create a self-sufficient system that automatically waters crops, adjusts shading based on weather conditions, and allows remote monitoring and control via the Internet.

Main features includes a moisture sensor-dependent automatic watering system to maintain ideal soil moisture levels ,a light sensor-dependent shade mechanism to protect plants from harsh sunlight ,and a remote control watering and shade mechanism as well as monitoring via the Internet for convenience and accessibility.

The project won a 'Best Exhibit Award' as it was presented in the IOT Cup UPD DCS 2024.

ISKO-INFO

IskoInfo is a comprehensive online platform designed to enhance the academic experience for students at the University of the Philippines Diliman. The system consists of several key sections, including Departments post aggregation, a masterlist of links to university resources, and a registration helper. It helps simplify information access, registration processes, and communication within the university community, making it an essential tool for students navigating their academic journey.

GENRE PREDICTOR

The project aims to predict multiple genres for movies based on their overviews. Using a dataset of over 9,000 movie descriptions from Kaggle, the team fine-tuned the DistilBERT model for multi-label classification. The model achieved an accuracy of 95.79%, with room for improvement in handling imbalanced genres and further fine-tuning​

DATA SCIENCE

This project analyzes misinformation about Cory Aquino's presidency and the EDSA Revolution during the 2022 Philippine elections. The team collected 150 tweets (2016-2022), used LDA topic modeling to identify false claims, and conducted statistical analysis. The findings suggests that there was no significant difference in topic distributions across election periods, suggesting no organized misinformation campaign.​

BALL TOSS
